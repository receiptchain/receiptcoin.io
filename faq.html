FAQ

500 Million
I sent money to the sale. How can I see my tokens?
In some apps like Delta (RCT), and Delta (RC) you will see your tokens immediately. In others like MyEtherWallet and Ethereum Wallet you will need to add the token in the custom token section. The token watch address is 0xd6e49800dECb64C0e195F791348C1e87a5864FD7
How many decimals is ReceiptCoin?
9
What is the symbol for ReceiptCoin? RC, RCT?
On Delta we are RCT. On imToken we are RC(ReciptCoin). On MyEtherWallet we are RC. On EtherDelta we are RCT.
Will there be more airdrops?
We only give airdrops out once to each email/twitter pair. Airdrops go out every 4 days or until presale is over.
I didn't get any tokens for the air drop.
Many people did not fill out both the email and twitter fields or inserted an incorrect ethereum address. If it has been more than 4 days please talk to an admin in the telegram channel. Please do not spam the channel. PM us directly.
When is presale over?
Presale is over at 11:00AM PST on Febrary 15th or until the presale reserve runs out.
Do I need to register for the token distribution event?
Yes, we will start official registration at the end of the presale. We will also email the registration information to everyone who has subscribed or participated in an airdrop or bounty.
What will the price of the tokens be during the general sale?
Approximately 0.000010 Ether / Token
